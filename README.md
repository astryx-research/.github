# 🛰️ ASTRYX Research Group

**A**I · **S**pace Technologies · **R**egulation / **R**esilience · C**y**bersecurity for Space Systems (**X**)

Welcome to the official GitHub organization for the ASTRYX Research Group. We study how intelligent, secure, and policy-aware technologies can strengthen the next generation of space infrastructure. Our work combines technical research, systems thinking, and policy analysis to protect and advance space systems.

---

## 🚀 Core Research Areas

* **AI for Space Systems:** Developing intelligent models for autonomous spacecraft operations, anomaly detection, mission planning, and resilient decision-making in orbit.
* **Space Technologies:** Exploring emerging architectures for satellite networks, space infrastructure, sensing, communications, and distributed orbital systems.
* **Regulation & Resilience:** Studying policy, governance, risk, and resilience frameworks for rapidly evolving space technologies and space-based critical infrastructure.
* **Cybersecurity for Space Systems:** Securing satellites, ground stations, command links, space data pipelines, and cyber-physical mission operations against modern threats.

## 📂 Featured Initiatives

* **IRIS-A (Integrated Resilience Integrity System for Autonomy):** Telemetry anomaly detection for communications, navigation, and tracking (CNT) systems.
* **Autonomous Constellation Security:** AI-driven threat detection models for distributed orbital systems.
* **Propulsion & Mechanical Systems:** Research into emerging technologies for deep space flight and spaceflight propulsion capabilities.
* **Risk Mitigation & Mission Assurance:** Development of automated testing systems, risk logs, and matrix strategies for flight systems.
